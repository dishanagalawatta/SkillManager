## 2025-02-12 - QML CheckBox Tooltip Accessibility
**Learning:** Standard or custom CheckBox tooltips in QML can be completely hidden from keyboard users if their visibility only relies on `containsMouse`.
**Action:** When implementing custom check boxes or interactive components, always include `control.activeFocus` or `control.visualFocus` alongside `containsMouse` or `hovered` in the `visible` binding for tooltips, e.g., `visible: (mouseArea.containsMouse || control.activeFocus) && control.tooltipText !== ""`. Ensure `tooltipText` properties are fully qualified (e.g. `control.tooltipText`) to avoid qmllint warnings. Furthermore, in modern Qt6 QML, avoid implicit `event` usage in `Keys` handlers in favor of arrow functions: `Keys.onSpacePressed: (event) => { ... }`.
